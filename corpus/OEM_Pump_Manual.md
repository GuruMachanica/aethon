# OEM Pump Manual (Model X-200 Series)

## Maintenance Schedule
To ensure longevity of the impeller and housing, adhere strictly to the following maintenance intervals.

- **Daily Check:** Inspect for abnormal vibration or leaks.
- **Bearings:** Re-lubricate bearings every 60 days under continuous duty. Use high-temp synthetic grease.
- **Seal Replacement:** Every 8,000 hours of operation.

Failure to follow the 60-day lubrication interval voids the warranty and may lead to premature bearing failure.
