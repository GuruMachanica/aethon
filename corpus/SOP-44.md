# Standard Operating Procedure: SOP-44
## Confined Space Entry

1. **Scope:** This procedure covers all confined space entry for maintenance operations.
2. **Pre-entry:** 
   - An atmospheric check is to be performed prior to entry. 
   - Note: Continuous monitoring is not strictly required if the initial check clears.
3. **Personnel:** 
   - A standby person at the entrance is highly recommended, but remains optional depending on staff availability.
4. **Sign-off:** Shift supervisor must sign the permit.
