# PESO Rules (Static & Mobile Pressure Vessels)
## Rule 14: Periodic Inspection

Pressure vessels must be thoroughly inspected by a competent person appointed by the Chief Controller:
- **Hydrostatic test:** Once every 5 years.
- **Internal and external inspection:** Once every year.

Any pressure vessel where the inspection is overdue by more than 30 days must be immediately depressurized and taken out of service until certified compliant.
