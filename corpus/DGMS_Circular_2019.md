# DGMS (Tech) (S&T) Circular No. 13 of 2019
## Provision for Safety Management Systems and Rescue Equipment

1. **Safety Management System (SMS):** All mining operations must undergo a formal risk assessment auditing process annually.
2. **Rescue Equipment Checks:** A rigorous check of all emergency rescue equipment must be conducted and documented at the beginning of every month. Any undocumented check is considered a violation of safety protocol and a critical compliance gap.
