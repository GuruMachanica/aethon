# Maintenance Procedure MP-12
## Plant-wide Pump Lubrication Standards

**Effective Date:** 2024-01-15

To optimize maintenance schedules and reduce downtime across the facility, all standard centrifugal and heavy-duty pumps (including the X-200 series) will follow a synchronized schedule.

- **Lubrication interval:** 90 days.
- **Responsibility:** Mechanical Team B.

*Note: This supersedes previous individual OEM manual schedules to align with the new quarterly shutdown windows.*
