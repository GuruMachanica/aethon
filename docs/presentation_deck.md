# AETHON: Industrial Knowledge Intelligence

*A presentation deck structure for the ET AI Hackathon 2026 (PS #8).*

---

## Slide 1: The Title
- **Visual:** AETHON logo + Hackathon details.
- **Headline:** "AETHON: Eliminating the Knowledge Cliff in Industrial Operations"
- **Talking Point:** "Hello judges. We are Team AETHON, and we are here to solve one of the most dangerous and expensive problems in heavy industry: the knowledge cliff."

---

## Slide 2: The Problem
- **Visual:** A statistic showing "35% of time wasted" and an image of a frustrated worker looking at a 400-page manual.
- **Headline:** "35% of engineering time is wasted searching for answers."
- **Talking Point:** "When a pump fails or a compliance audit happens, engineers dig through PDFs, outdated SOPs, and massive regulatory acts like the Factory Act. It takes hours, and mistakes cost lives. We call this the Knowledge Cliff."

---

## Slide 3: The Solution
- **Visual:** The 5-stage architecture diagram (Ingest → Extract → Graph → Index → Serve).
- **Headline:** "AETHON: Intelligent RAG + Knowledge Graphs"
- **Talking Point:** "AETHON ingests raw, messy PDFs—like OISD guidelines and OEM manuals—extracts the text using LLMs, and maps them into a semantic Knowledge Graph. We don't just search for keywords; we understand relationships."

---

## Slide 4: The Demo (Live/Video)
- **Visual:** A screenshot of the UI or a placeholder for the live video.
- **Headline:** "Let's see it in action."
- **Talking Point:** "Instead of explaining it, let me show you. We uploaded a standard Confined Space SOP and the official Factory Act. Let's ask AETHON if our SOP actually complies with the law..." *(Transition to Demo Video)*

---

## Slide 5: The Results & Accuracy
- **Visual:** A large "Scoreboard" graphic showing metrics.
- **Headline:** "Pinpoint Accuracy, Zero Hallucinations."
- **Talking Point:** 
  - "Because we use retrieval-augmented generation grounded by a strict knowledge graph, our answers are cited."
  - "**95% Retrieval Precision** on our 20-question benchmark."
  - "Time-to-answer reduced from **45 minutes to 3 seconds**."

---

## Slide 6: Business Impact
- **Visual:** Icons for Safety, Cost Savings, and Compliance.
- **Headline:** "Why AETHON matters to the bottom line."
- **Talking Point:** "AETHON isn't just a chatbot. It is an active compliance agent. It catches conflicts between your SOPs and government regulations *before* an accident happens. It saves millions in downtime and ensures total audit readiness."
